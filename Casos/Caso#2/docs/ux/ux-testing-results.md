## Acceptance Criteria

UX testing is considered complete when:

- At least 4 external design students complete the test.
- Every participant attempts the main verification flow.
- Results are documented in the results table.
- Detected issues are classified by severity.
- Corrections are documented with a clear reason.
- The Figma prototype is updated based on the findings.
- The updated prototype still supports text, URL, and image/screenshot verification.
- The prototype clearly shows `PASS`, `NO_PASS`, and `HUMAN_REVIEW`.
- The prototype does not use `TRUE` or `FALSE` as final result labels.
